Keeping your home as dry as possible will help keep mold and mildew away and make it a healthier place to be. We have dehumidifiers that can help take moisture out of the air in your house, collecting it and storing it in a bucket or reservoir until you need it outside

This dehumidifier is for use in small rooms or areas up to 600 square feet. It's great for basements, home offices, RVs and boats. It is lightweight and portable, making it easy to move from one room to another as needed. Simple operation includes a handle on top of the unit for carrying and casters on the bottom of the unit to allow easy moving. It's also quiet so it won't disturb you while you sleep.
